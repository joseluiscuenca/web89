José Luis Cuenca Moncada
Guayaquil, Guayas [Ecuador], 1989.

University Degrees_

PhD in Communication and Cultural Industries – Polytechnic University of Valencia, Department of Audiovisual Communication, Documentation and History of Art (Valencia, Spain, 2019 - 2024) with the thesis ’Analysis of 3D Stereoscopic Methods in the Animation's field‘.(Cum Laude).

Master's Degree in Design Engineering – Polytechnic University of Valencia, Higher Technical School of Design Engineering (Valencia, Spain, 2019).

Master's Degree in Multimedia and Visual Arts – Polytechnic University of Valencia, School of Fine Arts of San Carlos (Valencia, Spain, 2018 - 2020).

Master's Degree in Audiovisual Posproduction – Polytechnic University of Valencia, Higher Polytechnic School of Gandia (Valencia, Spain, 2017).

Bachelor of Design and Audiovisual Production – Higher Polytechnic School of the Litoral, School of Visual Design and Comunication (Guayaquil, Guayas , Ecuador, 2009 - 2017).

He has conducted research in the fields of New Media, Sound Art, Visual Art, Audiovisual Production, Read & Write about The Digital Culture. His work has focused on the new creative possibilities of Digital Graphic and New Media Art.

 

Art Works / Exhibitions_

As a multimedia artist, his art work has been part of prestigious exhibitions and festivals such as Ars Electronica (Linz, Austria, 2019), Art Museum of Pereira (Colombia, 2018), Contemporary Art Center of Quito (CAC-2018), Interactos International Festival (Ecuador, 2017), + Arte Gallery (Ecuador, 2017), Arte Actual FLACSO (Ecuador, 2016), the International Video Art & Video-Performance Festival INTERCAMBIOS (Colombia, 2016), the International Video Art Festival nodoCCS (Venezuela, 2015), UNESCO International Arts Education Week (Brussels, Belgium, 2015), The Dub Dub copy (Puebla, Mexico, 2014), the II International Festival of Contemporary Engraving INGRÁFICA (Cuenca, Spain, 2009) and the XVI International Fair of Contemporary Multiple Art ESTAMPA (Madrid, Spain, 2008), among others.
